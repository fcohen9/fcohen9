About

I am currently studying graphic design at the Maryland Institute College of Art. My practice skews mostly towards UI and Motion, although I am still comfortable with more traditional print design and systems. I am proficient with Adobe suite as well as other prototyping products such as Figma and Sketch. I spend a lot of my free time coding because I love being able to execute things from beginning to end, and also because my knowledge of this subject is helpful when talking with non-designers, especially in tech-related fields. I love doing what I do because I love coming up with big picture solutions, but I also get excited by all the smaller parts, like creating complex grids and style sheets.
