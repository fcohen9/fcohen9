Blair Witch Project Animated Posters

Motion Posters Based on The Blair Witch Project

process

For this project, I was inspired by Wong Pingâ€™s Animations, which use strange colors and illustration styles, gradients, and interesting ways of animating.

deliverables

This project posed some challenges, as my vision required animating techniques that are beyond the scope of design. I had to think of another way to move all the pieces in my posters. Since I was already trying to do something with a post internet aesthetic, I decided to use the cursor as a means to animate my posters. It was difficult to get all the movement to look like it was on a computer and caused by the cursor, but it was a good solution that solved my issues with how to animate my posters and allowed me to include all the elements that I wanted to include.
